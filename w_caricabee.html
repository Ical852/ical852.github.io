And there are 2 way that i know to scrapping a product from other website, and you can see how to do it by clicking the show button.

Scrap Product

Scrapped Product

CariCabee Shop

This is one of the other way to scrap product form another website. That it doesn't need database to show the scrap product. It means the scrapped product is directly show when you open the page. But the lack of this way is you need more time to load the page. Because the system have to scrap all of the product first to show it on the page. But the excess of this way is you don't need to update the scrapped product data routinely, because it will updated automatically if origin website is updated the product data.

Scrap to Database

CariCabee Shop

Scrap Product data to databse is another way to scrapping product to our website. The different is the product data that we want to scrap is must be inputted to the database first and then select from database to show the product data to the page. The excess is faster loading page because the product data is in our database. But the lack is we have to update the scrapped data routinely because it doesn't updated automatically.
